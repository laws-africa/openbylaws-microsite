<span class="akn-hcontainer" id="att_1/sec_2__hcontainer_1" data-eId="sec_2__hcontainer_1" data-name="hcontainer"><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">A member must attend each meeting of the council and of a committee as contemplated in section 10 of this by-law.</span></span></span></section><section class="akn-section" id="att_1/sec_3" data-eId="sec_3"><h3>3. Leave of absence</h3>
<span class="akn-hcontainer" id="att_1/sec_3__hcontainer_1" data-eId="sec_3__hcontainer_1" data-name="hcontainer"><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">Applications for leave of absence must be dealt with in terms of section 11 of this by-law.</span></span></span></section><section class="akn-section" id="att_1/sec_4" data-eId="sec_4"><h3>4. Reporting of non-attendance</h3>
<span class="akn-hcontainer" id="att_1/sec_4__hcontainer_1" data-eId="sec_4__hcontainer_1" data-name="hcontainer"><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">The municipal manager must report each case of non-attendance to the speaker not later than the next working day after the meeting concerned.</span></span></span></section><section class="akn-section" id="att_1/sec_5" data-eId="sec_5"><h3>5. Duty of the speaker</h3>
<section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_5__subsec_1" data-eId="sec_5__subsec_1"><span class="akn-num">(1)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">The speaker must, within two working days, after receipt of the report of non-attendance from the municipal manager, inform the member concerned in writing of the report and request the written comments of the member within seven working days of the date of notification.</span></span></section><section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_5__subsec_2" data-eId="sec_5__subsec_2"><span class="akn-num">(2)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">Upon receipt of the comments of the member, or, if the member fails to furnish the speaker with his or her comments within the seven working days, or after such extension of time that may be permitted by the speaker, the speaker must convene a meeting of the Attendance Committee and instruct the member concerned in writing to attend such meeting.</span></span></section></section><section class="akn-section" id="att_1/sec_6" data-eId="sec_6"><h3>6. Procedures at the Attendance Committee meeting</h3>
<section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_1" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_1"><span class="akn-num">(1)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-blockList" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_1__list_1" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_1__list_1"><span class="akn-listIntroduction">The speaker must determine the procedures for the hearing of the matter which must include the following–</span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_1__list_1__item_a" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_1__list_1__item_a"><span class="akn-num">(a)</span><span class="akn-p">that the member concerned has the right to be present when any evidence is heard and may ask questions based on the report of the municipal manager;</span></span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_1__list_1__item_b" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_1__list_1__item_b"><span class="akn-num">(b)</span><span class="akn-p">that the members of the Attendance Committee may ask questions to the member; and</span></span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_1__list_1__item_c" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_1__list_1__item_c"><span class="akn-num">(c)</span><span class="akn-p">that the member shall have the right to adduce evidence and to call witnesses.-</span></span></span></span></section><section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_2" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_2"><span class="akn-num">(2)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-blockList" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_2__list_1" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_2__list_1"><span class="akn-listIntroduction">If the member–</span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_2__list_1__item_a" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_2__list_1__item_a"><span class="akn-num">(a)</span><span class="akn-p">fails or refuses to attend the meeting; or</span></span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_2__list_1__item_b" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_2__list_1__item_b"><span class="akn-num">(b)</span><span class="akn-p">is requested to leave because of his or her conduct at the meeting, the meeting may continue in his or her absence.</span></span></span></span></section><section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_3" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_3"><span class="akn-num">(3)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">The Attendance Committee may call witnesses to assist in making a decision.</span></span></section><section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_6__subsec_4" data-eId="sec_6__subsec_4"><span class="akn-num">(4)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">If the speaker is the person accused of non-attendance, then the executive mayor must chair the Attendance Committee.</span></span></section></section><section class="akn-section" id="att_1/sec_7" data-eId="sec_7"><h3>7. Findings of the Attendance Committee</h3>
<section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_7__subsec_1" data-eId="sec_7__subsec_1"><span class="akn-num">(1)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">After considering all the evidence contemplated in item 6, the Attendance Committee must make a finding as to whether the member contravened section 10(2) of this by-law, and record its reasons for the finding.</span></span></section><section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_7__subsec_2" data-eId="sec_7__subsec_2"><span class="akn-num">(2)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">If the finding is that the member did not contravene any of the provisions contemplated in sub section (1), the speaker must adjourn the meeting and inform him or her of the finding in writing.</span></span></section><section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_7__subsec_3" data-eId="sec_7__subsec_3"><span class="akn-num">(3)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">If the finding is that the member has contravened section 10(2) of this by-law, the</span><span class="akn-p">Attendance Committee must impose the relevant sanction as contemplated in item 9 and the speaker must, in writing, advise the municipal manager and the member accordingly.</span></span></section><section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_7__subsec_4" data-eId="sec_7__subsec_4"><span class="akn-num">(4)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">The municipal manager must take the necessary steps to ensure payment of any fine imposed or to record any other sanction against the name of the member.</span></span></section><section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_7__subsec_5" data-eId="sec_7__subsec_5"><span class="akn-num">(5)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">If the finding is that the member was absent from three or more council meetings or committee meetings of which he or she is a member, the speaker must, within seven working days, submit a report to that effect to council to be dealt with in terms of item 4(2) of the Code of Conduct.</span></span></section></section><section class="akn-section" id="att_1/sec_8" data-eId="sec_8"><h3>8. Consideration of the report from the Attendance Committee</h3>
<section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_8__subsec_1" data-eId="sec_8__subsec_1"><span class="akn-num">(1)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">If the council agrees with the findings of the Attendance Committee contemplated in item 7(5), the MEC must, within seven working days after such resolution, be requested to remove the member from office.</span></span></section><section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_8__subsec_2" data-eId="sec_8__subsec_2"><span class="akn-num">(2)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">If the council disagrees with the report of the Attendance Committee, it must instigate its own investigation into the matter within seven working days after such resolution.</span></span></section></section><section class="akn-section" id="att_1/sec_9" data-eId="sec_9"><h3>9. <section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_9__subsec_1" data-eId="sec_9__subsec_1"><span class="akn-num">(1)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-blockList" id="att_1/sec_9__subsec_1__list_1" data-eId="sec_9__subsec_1__list_1"><span class="akn-listIntroduction">The Attendance Committee may, upon finding of a contravention of section 10(2) of this by-law, impose the following sanctions–</span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_9__subsec_1__list_1__item_a" data-eId="sec_9__subsec_1__list_1__item_a"><span class="akn-num">(a)</span><span class="akn-p">for non-attendance, or failure to remain in attendance at a meeting, impose a fine equal to 5% of the member’s monthly gross salary package, excluding allowances;</span></span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_9__subsec_1__list_1__item_b" data-eId="sec_9__subsec_1__list_1__item_b"><span class="akn-num">(b)</span><span class="akn-p">for non-attendance, or failure to remain in attendance at two consecutive meetings, impose a fine equal to 10% of the member’s monthly gross salary package, excluding allowances;</span></span></span></span></section><section class="akn-subsection" id="att_1/sec_9__subsec_2" data-eId="sec_9__subsec_2"><span class="akn-num">(2)</span><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">Absence from three or more executive council or committee meetings shall be dealt with in terms of Item 7(5) of this Schedule.</span></span></section></section><section class="akn-section" id="att_1/sec_10" data-eId="sec_10"><h3>10. Exemptions</h3>
<span class="akn-hcontainer" id="att_1/sec_10__hcontainer_1" data-eId="sec_10__hcontainer_1" data-name="hcontainer"><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-blockList" id="att_1/sec_10__hcontainer_1__list_1" data-eId="sec_10__hcontainer_1__list_1"><span class="akn-listIntroduction">Members shall be exempted from the provisions of this Schedule in cases of absence as result of–</span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_10__hcontainer_1__list_1__item_a" data-eId="sec_10__hcontainer_1__list_1__item_a"><span class="akn-num">(a)</span><span class="akn-p">illness, provided that an acceptable medical certificate is submitted to the speaker;</span></span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_10__hcontainer_1__list_1__item_b" data-eId="sec_10__hcontainer_1__list_1__item_b"><span class="akn-num">(b)</span><span class="akn-p">death or trauma in the family, provided that a sworn statement be submitted;</span></span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_10__hcontainer_1__list_1__item_c" data-eId="sec_10__hcontainer_1__list_1__item_c"><span class="akn-num">(c)</span><span class="akn-p">official business of the council; or</span></span><span class="akn-item" id="att_1/sec_10__hcontainer_1__list_1__item_d" data-eId="sec_10__hcontainer_1__list_1__item_d"><span class="akn-num">(d)</span><span class="akn-p">approval of absence by the speaker in terms of section 11 of this by-law;</span></span></span></span></span></section><section class="akn-section" id="att_1/sec_11" data-eId="sec_11"><h3>11. Appeal</h3>
<span class="akn-hcontainer" id="att_1/sec_11__hcontainer_1" data-eId="sec_11__hcontainer_1" data-name="hcontainer"><span class="akn-content"><span class="akn-p">A member against whom sanctions have been imposed in terms of item 9(1)(a) or (b) of this Schedule, may within 14 days of having been notified of the decision of the Attendance Committee, appeal to the council in writing against the finding as well the sanction imposed, setting out the reasons on which the appeal is based.</span></span></span></section></span></span>
